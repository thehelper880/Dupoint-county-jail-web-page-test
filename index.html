Roles Guide

Cop

Protect Dupoint County by enforcing the law. Arrest criminals, patrol the streets, and keep the peace. Use your weapons and vehicles wisely, and coordinate with your team.

Criminal

Plan your escape or cause chaos. Work with fellow criminals to break out of jail, rob banks, and avoid cops. Use stealth and teamwork to survive.

Judge

Oversee court hearings and ensure justice is served. Decide on punishments and keep the game fair. Your decisions affect the balance between cops and criminals.

Gameplay Tips

  • Cops: Communicate often and use vehicles to cover ground quickly.
  • Criminals: Use distractions and coordinate jailbreaks for the best success.
  • Everyone: Respect the roleplay rules and have fun!
